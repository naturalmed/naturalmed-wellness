About Nuno

Practitioner of
Chinese Medicine

"It is my mission to look after you and provide an alternative solution to your health problems."

Nuno graduated from Chengdu University of Traditional Chinese Medicine — one of the oldest TCM universities in China — where he studied, interned in a hospital, and honed his clinical skills alongside his Mandarin Chinese. He has been practising acupuncture across the UK for over a decade, now based at his clinic in Newtown, Powys.

He specialises in acupuncture and has achieved significant results treating both acute and chronic conditions. Beyond his clinical work, Nuno is a scholar and researcher of Chinese medicine and culture.
