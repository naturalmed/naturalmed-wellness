Privacy Policy & Cookies

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Version 1.0

NaturalMed (operated by Nuno Pestana) is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the UK GDPR and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. Data Controller

Nuno Pestana — NaturalMed Wellness
30 Shortbridge Street, Newtown, Powys SY16 2LN, United Kingdom
Email: [email protected]
Phone: +44 7756 339 382

2. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data only when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Contact form: First name, last name, email address, phone number (optional), subject, and message.
  • Newsletter subscription: Email address only.
  • Analytics (with consent): Anonymised usage data via Google Analytics — pages visited, time on site, browser type, and anonymised IP address.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except as described in this policy or as required by law.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contact form / newsletter: Your explicit request to be contacted (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR — performance of a pre-contractual relationship).
  • Essential cookies: Legitimate interest to operate the website (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Analytics cookies: Your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use the minimum number necessary.

4.1 Essential Cookies (always active)

Purpose: Store your cookie-consent preference so we don't ask again.

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

4.2 Analytics Cookies (only with your consent)

Provider: Google Analytics 4

Purpose: Understand how visitors navigate the site so we can improve it.

Data collected: Pages visited, session duration, browser type, anonymised IP.

Retention: 26 months.

Legal basis: Your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).

6. Your Rights Under UK/EU GDPR

  • Right of Access — Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification — Correct inaccurate data we hold.
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing — Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability — Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Withdraw cookie consent at any time below.

To exercise any of these rights, contact: [email protected]

8. Data Retention

Contact form enquiries and newsletter email addresses are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your request or until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained for 26 months in Google Analytics.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes. Material changes that affect your rights will trigger a renewed consent request.

11. Supervisory Authority

If you are based in the UK you may contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). If you are based in the EU you may contact your local data protection authority.
